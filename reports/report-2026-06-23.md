### 🧠 Builder Tip  

When applying to grants or hackathons tied to a *specific* stack (e.g., Mantle, Solana, X Layer, Monad), ship a **minimal but complete technical artifact** (e.g., a working agent, a single market call, or a gasless UI) *before* submitting. This lets you talk about actual performance, risk, and UX in your pitch, not hypotheticals—and signals quality when funds are constrained.
