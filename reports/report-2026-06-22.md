### 🧠 Builder Tip
Apply with a **tight, evidence-backed scope**: one problem, one user, one measurable outcome, and one short demo. For hackathons and grants alike, reviewers reward projects that clearly show **why this now, why you, and what ships in 2–4 weeks**—not broad roadmaps. [1][15]
