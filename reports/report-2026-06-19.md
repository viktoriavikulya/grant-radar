## 🛰️ Grant Radar — 2026-06-19

Activity is concentrated in **bounties** and **hackathon-style builder contests**, with a smaller but steady stream of **grant-tracking and grant-discovery tooling**. - **Grant workflow automation** — the `ncreighton/*` repos around grant writing, donor tracking, and deadline alerts signal demand for internal tooling that reduces admin overhead for nonprofits and universities.[12][13][14] Developers with SaaS, automation, or CRM-adjacent chops should look here because the use case is concrete and deadline-driven.[12][13][14]

### 🧠 Builder Tip
Apply with a **narrow, outcome-first pitch**: state the exact problem, the measurable deliverable, and why your implementation is faster or more reliable than a generic demo. For bounty and hackathon programs, include a short proof-of-work artifact—scope analysis, test cases, or a working prototype—because the strongest signals here reward *execution speed* and *program fit*.[2][5][7]
